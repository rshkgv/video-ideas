#lakers

Парад чемпионов

Городская улица между небоскрёбами, конфетти сыплется, как снег. Пользователь едет на открытой платформе парадного кортежа, машет толпе вдоль тротуаров — камера синхронно скользит вдоль машины, ровно на её скорости, не отставая ни на кадр. Постепенно улыбка становится шире, движения — увереннее. В финале с фасада высотки разворачивается гигантский вымпел с надписью «CHAMPIONS» — и остаётся висеть до конца видео. Никакой арены — только город, отмечающий победу вместе с командой.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a championship parade moving down a city street lined with skyscrapers. The person rides on an open-top float, medium shot, confetti falling steadily around them.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera performs one continuous lateral tracking shot for the entire video, moving alongside the float at its exact speed — never speeding up, slowing down, or cutting:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: The float moves at a steady pace down the street. The person waves modestly, confetti drifting lightly. Skyscrapers and blurred crowd shapes slide past in the background.
2. FRAMES 20–60%: The tracking continues at the same steady speed. Confetti thickens in the air. The person's waving grows bigger and more joyful, turning more directly toward the camera, grin widening.
3. FRAMES 60–85%: The person raises both arms, fully immersed in the moment, confetti now dense in the air between the camera and the float. The tracking shot holds its speed, smooth and unbroken.
4. FRAMES 85–100%: The tracking shot gently settles to match the person centered in frame. A giant pennant banner reading "CHAMPIONS 🏆" in purple-and-gold unfurls down the face of a skyscraper in the background and remains hanging, fully visible, until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, happening only once: modest wave → widening joy → arms raised in full celebration. No other movement.

Key visual events (in order):
- The float and camera moving in perfect lockstep down the street (the continuity spine of the shot)
- Confetti density increasing steadily throughout
- The pennant banner unfurling down the skyscraper at the very end (the hero moment)

Style:
Bright, celebratory, civic-scale spectacle — closer to a real city championship parade broadcast than an arena broadcast. Warm midday light, purple-and-gold confetti against glass-and-steel skyscrapers. Joyful, communal, triumphant.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- The camera must move as one single continuous tracking shot at a constant speed matching the float — no cuts, no speed changes, no handheld shake
- The "CHAMPIONS 🏆" banner appears only in the final 15% and must remain fully visible, unmoving, until the last frame
- Do not depict any specific real athlete's face or likeness anywhere in the scene — the float and crowd stay generic, no individual player call-outs
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media UI overlays
- No text captions other than the "CHAMPIONS 🏆" banner
- Confetti only — no pyrotechnics or fire
- No violent or aggressive actions
