Замах на поле для гольфа

Солнечный день, поле для гольфа — та самая непринуждённая тусовка друзей, в которой любят проводить время звёзды вроде Кевина Харта (сами друзья лишь угадываются на периферии). Пользователь готовится к удару с ти — камера очень медленно и почти незаметно наезжает, пока нарастает комичное напряжение: капля пота, нервный смешок, слишком серьёзная сосредоточенность для обычного удара. Разворот — абсурдно несерьёзный: мяч улетает по нелепой, неожиданной траектории (например, с крутым виражом), и пользователь взрывается смехом над самим собой, не разочарованием. Лёгкая, тёплая комедийная зарисовка без сцены и зрителей.

The scene is a sunny golf course tee box. The person stands in a medium shot, mid-stance over the ball, gripping a club with exaggerated seriousness.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera performs one extremely slow, barely perceptible continuous push-in for the entire video — the slowness itself builds comic tension:
1. FRAMES 0–35%: The person is frozen in an overly serious golf stance, staring down at the ball as if it were a life-or-death moment. A single bead of sweat forms and slowly rolls down their temple. Their jaw is clenched with comic over-seriousness.
2. FRAMES 35–60%: The tension keeps building — a nervous half-smile flickers, a quick anxious glance up and back down at the ball, shoulders tightening further. The slow push-in continues, tightening the frame around their face and the club.
3. FRAMES 60–80%: The person finally swings — a single clean, committed motion. Immediately after, their face shifts to a comic wince of anticipation, eyes squeezed half-shut, waiting to see what happens.
4. FRAMES 80–100%: Whatever happened is clearly absurd and harmless (implied by the person's reaction, not shown as a separate shot) — the person's face bursts into surprised, delighted laughter at their own results, shoulders shaking, completely undone by their own swing. No overlay graphics — the moment is carried entirely by the reaction.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, happening only once: exaggerated serious stance → building nervous tension (sweat, glances) → the swing → comic wince → surprised self-directed laughter. No other movement or gestures.

Key visual events (in order):
- The single slow bead of sweat rolling down the temple during the tension build
- The committed golf swing — one clean motion, no repeats
- The comic wince immediately after impact
- The burst of genuine self-directed laughter as the payoff

Style:
Warm, sunlit, observational comedy — think a dry mockumentary close-up on an ordinary moment played with theatrical over-seriousness, in the same laid-back celebrity-golf-day spirit associated with Kevin Hart's off-stage persona. No music-video gloss; grounded, naturalistic golf-course lighting with a gently comic tone.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point

- No other people clearly visible or detailed in the frame — any implied friends must stay entirely out of frame or fully unrendered
