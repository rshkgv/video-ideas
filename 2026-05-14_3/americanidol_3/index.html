#americanidol

Перед дверью

Никакой сцены, никакой толпы — только гулкая пустая лестничная клетка за кулисами American Idol. Пользователь один, тихо распевается перед выходом, глаза закрыты, рука на холодной стене. Камера статична, затем — предельно медленный наезд, пока человек делает один шаг к двери, из-под которой пробивается тёплый свет. Видео обрывается ровно в момент, когда рука касается ручки двери — за дверью ничего не показано. Ни титров, ни оверлеев: тишина важнее спецэффектов.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a quiet, empty backstage stairwell on American Idol finale night. The person from the image stands alone, eyes closed, doing a silent vocal warm-up.

Camera and motion structure:
1. FRAMES 0–45%: Locked-off static medium shot. The person stands still, eyes closed, taking slow, deliberate breaths. Their lips move very subtly in a silent hum — no audible words, just breath and small mouth shapes. One hand rests against the wall for grounding.
2. FRAMES 45–70%: The person opens their eyes and takes one single, deliberate step toward the door at the end of the corridor. The sliver of light beneath the door grows almost imperceptibly brighter and warmer as they approach.
3. FRAMES 70–100%: An extremely slow, deliberate push-in follows the person as they reach the door and rest their hand on the handle. The video ends exactly as their hand touches the handle and the door begins to crack open, warm light just beginning to spill onto their face — the space beyond the door is never shown. Freeze on this threshold moment.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, each stage happening only once: stillness and silent breathing → a single step forward → hand reaching the door handle → the door beginning to crack open. Understated throughout — no big gesture, no smile-to-camera, no performance energy.

Key visual events (in order):
- The silent vocal warm-up — breath and subtle mouth shapes only
- The single step toward the door
- The light beneath the door growing almost imperceptibly brighter
- The door beginning to crack open at the very last frame, light just starting to spill across the person's face

Style:
Quiet, intimate, observational documentary realism. Cool stairwell tones giving way to one warm light source. No music-video bombast, no broadcast energy — the opposite of a triumphant reveal.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- No overlay graphics, titles, or text of any kind anywhere in this video
- No visible stage, audience, confetti, or any performance space — stairwell and door only
- The door must remain no more than barely cracked open by the final frame — never show what is beyond it
- No speech and no audible words — only breath and silent lip movement
- Exactly one camera move — the slow push-in during the final third — no whip, no fast cuts, no additional movement
- No other people visible in the stairwell
