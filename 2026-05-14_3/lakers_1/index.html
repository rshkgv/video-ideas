#lakers_1

Экстренная новость

Обычная кухня, будний день — пользователь сидит за столом, телефон лежит рядом экраном вниз, на фоне тихо бормочет спортивное радио. Из динамика вдруг отчётливо звучит голос ведущего: подтверждение громкой новости о команде. В ту же секунду на экран влетает жирная плашка-молния «CONFIRMED», а лицо пользователя мгновенно взрывается недоверчивой, но абсолютно счастливой улыбкой. Никакой арены, никакой толпы — просто обычный фанат и одна оглушительная секунда.

Turn the photo of the person into a candid medium shot of them sitting at a plain kitchen table on an ordinary afternoon, wearing a casual purple-and-gold Lakers T-shirt, a phone lying face-down beside their hand, warm natural window light, a slightly tense and expectant expression.
Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is an ordinary kitchen on a quiet afternoon. The person sits at the table, phone face-down beside them, wearing a purple-and-gold Lakers T-shirt.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is completely locked off for the entire video — no movement, no zoom:
1. FRAMES 0–30%: The person waits, restless — fingers tapping the table once, eyes flicking to the phone and back. A sports-radio host's voice murmurs indistinctly in the background, barely audible.
2. FRAMES 30–55%: The radio voice sharpens and grows clearer, mid-sentence, building toward an announcement. The person goes still, leaning very slightly toward the sound, listening intently.
3. FRAMES 55–65%: The instant the announcer's voice lands on the confirmation, a bold "CONFIRMED 🏆" news-flash graphic slams onto the screen with a hard impact effect — a Lakers purple-and-gold banner with a small gold shield mark.
4. FRAMES 65–100%: The person's face bursts from disbelief into an explosive grin, hands flying up to their head then punching the air once. The graphic remains locked on screen, unmoving, until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, happening only once: restless waiting → stillness/listening → shock → explosive joy. No other movement.

Key visual events (in order):
- The radio host's voice sharpening from murmur to clear announcement (the only dialogue in the video)
- The synchronized graphic-slam + expression-break at FRAME 55–65% (the hero moment)
- The explosive, silent celebratory reaction

Style:
Naturalistic, handheld-free, grounded documentary realism — an ordinary room, ordinary light, one extraordinary second. The only "broadcast" element is the news-flash graphic itself.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- The camera must remain completely static throughout — no pans, no zoom, no handheld motion
- The radio host is the only voice heard; the person never speaks, mouths words, or lip-syncs at any point
- Do not display any readable text on the phone screen — it stays face-down and out of focus throughout
- The "CONFIRMED 🏆" graphic appears only at FRAME 55–65% and must remain fully visible, unmoving, until the last frame
- Do not name, depict, or imply any specific real player or transaction detail — the news stays generic ("a landmark roster move"), never a fabricated specific trade
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media UI overlays
- No other people in frame
- No violent or aggressive actions
