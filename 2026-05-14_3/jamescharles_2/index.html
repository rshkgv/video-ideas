#jamescharles_2

Читаю комментарии

Пользователь сидит в мягком свете экрана телефона (сам экран не показан) и листает комментарии под своим последним видео — в духе бьюти-блогеров, которые снимают "реакцию на комментарии" как отдельный формат. Никакой трансформации: только нарастающая цепочка эмоций — удивление, хохот, растроганность. Чистая, комедийная, узнаваемая зарисовка из жизни инфлюенсера.

Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a cozy home setting at night. The person from the image sits on a couch, their face lit by the soft glow of an implied phone screen held off-frame below.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is completely static throughout — a locked close-up, no zoom, no reveal:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: The person scrolls with a calm, curious expression, screen-glow flickering gently on their face as if new comments are loading.
2. FRAMES 20–45%: Their eyebrows shoot up in surprise at something they read — a small gasp, head pulling back slightly.
3. FRAMES 45–70%: Surprise breaks into genuine delighted laughter — head tips back, eyes crinkle, a real unguarded laugh.
4. FRAMES 70–100%: The laughter settles into a soft, touched smile — a hand comes up to their chest, eyes glossy with emotion, clearly moved by something kind they read. Screen-glow holds steady on their face until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, happening only once: calm scrolling → surprise → delighted laughter → touched, emotional smile. This is a pure reaction sequence — no transformation, no environment change, no reveal of any kind.

Key visual events (in order):
- The screen-glow flicker suggesting new comments loading
- The surprise gasp
- The full unguarded laugh (the hero moment — the most genuine, least performed beat)
- The quiet, touched smile at the end

Style:
Warm, intimate, handheld-feeling realism despite the locked camera — like a candid moment caught mid-scroll. Cool phone-glow against warm ambient room light. No spectacle, no overlay graphics — the emotional honesty of the reactions is the entire content.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- No phone or screen visible in frame — only its implied glow and light flicker on the face
- No text captions, no comment bubbles, no visible UI of any kind
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media overlays
- The reaction arc happens only once, in order: surprise → laughter → touched — no looping or repeating beats
- No aggressive or violent actions
- No overlay graphics of any kind — this idea intentionally has no on-screen text or icon
