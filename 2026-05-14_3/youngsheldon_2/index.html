#youngsheldon

Последний золотой час

Пользователь сидит один на крыльце дома, тихо покачиваясь на качелях, со стаканом лимонада в руке. Ничего не происходит — и в этом всё дело: небо за его спиной медленно и непрерывно меняет цвет, от дневного золота к глубокому закатному пурпуру, будто сама природа неспешно перелистывает последнюю страницу. Ни разговоров, ни события — только тихое течение времени и лёгкая улыбка, обращённая куда-то за кадр. В конце — сдержанный, элегичный титр, как последние титры сериала, который прощается с домом, ставшим родным.

Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a quiet suburban front porch at golden hour. The person from the image sits alone on a wooden porch swing, holding a glass of lemonade, a soft-focus yard and street visible behind them.

Camera and motion structure:
A single slow, continuous push-in throughout, from medium shot toward a slightly closer medium-close-up — barely perceptible, never rushed.
FRAMES 0–30%: The person sits on the gently swaying porch swing, warm gold late-afternoon light. The sky behind them glows a warm gold.
FRAMES 30–65%: The sky gradually and continuously shifts color — gold fading into soft orange, then dusty rose — a slow, seamless gradient change, like a long exposure of dusk arriving. The person's expression stays calm; one slow blink, the gentle rocking of the swing continues unchanged.
FRAMES 65–85%: The sky deepens further into a soft dusty purple-blue as a single porch light bulb flickers on warmly beside them. The person takes one slow sip of lemonade, sets the glass down, gaze drifting toward the darkening street.
FRAMES 85–100%: The push-in settles to a stop. A soft, understated title card fades in at the lower third, small and muted gold serif: "One Last Golden Hour." It remains until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
The person's only motion: the slow rocking of the swing, one sip of lemonade, one gaze shift toward the street, one slow blink. No walking, no other motion, no speaking.

Key visual events (in order):
- The sky's slow, continuous color shift from gold to dusty purple (the hero moment — an environmental, not a facial, transformation)
- The porch light flickering on
- The single sip and gaze shift toward the street
- The quiet title card fading in

Style:
Warm, quiet, elegiac — closer to a valedictory montage than a broadcast celebration. Soft film grain, warm-to-cool color drift as the light fades. Understated, gentle, emotionally warm rather than dramatic.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's face, hair, skin tone, or identity at any point
- The sky's color transition must be continuous and gradual throughout — never a jump cut or instant change
- The person never speaks — no lip movement, no dialogue
- The title card appears only in the final 15% and must remain visible until the last frame
- No other people in frame
- No modern elements — no cars, smartphones, or anything visibly post-1980s in the street or yard
- No speech bubbles or captions other than the specified title card
- No social-media UI overlays
- No fast or jittery motion — every beat must read as slow and calm
- No aggressive or violent actions
