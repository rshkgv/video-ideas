#justgolive

Выходишь в прямой эфир

Визуальный якорь — типичная домашняя стрим-студия для TikTok LIVE: кольцевая лампа, телефон на мини-штативе, и в кадре — характерный красный значок «LIVE» плюс счётчик зрителей в углу экрана. Пользователь садится перед камерой, поправляет свет, и в момент начала трансляции значок «LIVE» вспыхивает, а по бокам экрана начинают взлетать вверх цветные сердечки и «подарки»-иконки, как будто зрители уже отправляют реакции. Пользователь широко, довольно улыбается, глядя в объектив — ни слова, только энергия момента.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is locked off, static, throughout — a single continuous take:
1. FRAMES 0–30%: The person makes a final small adjustment (touching their hair, angling the ring light slightly), anticipatory energy building, glancing at the implied phone screen and back to camera.
2. FRAMES 30–55%: The person settles, takes a breath, and looks directly into the camera with a bright, ready expression — the moment just before going live.
3. FRAMES 55–75%: A red "LIVE" badge flashes on in the top corner of the frame, and a small viewer-count number begins ticking upward rapidly beside it. The person's face lights up with excitement at the response.
4. FRAMES 75–100%: Colorful heart icons and small gift-box icons begin floating up from the bottom corners of the frame, increasing in density, as if a growing audience is reacting in real time. The person breaks into a wide, delighted smile, waving once at the camera — no words. Hold on this energetic moment to the last frame.

Character:

Key visual events (in order):
- The final adjustment and anticipatory build-up

- The floating hearts and gift icons increasing in density
- The person's delighted smile and silent wave at the end

Style:
Bright, energetic, ring-lit home-streaming realism — the LIVE badge and floating reaction icons are the visual signature, styled like real TikTok LIVE UI but original/generic rather than a literal screenshot of the app. Warm, celebratory momentum.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's face, hair, skin tone, or identity at any point
- The person's face must remain clearly visible and the dominant subject throughout — the ring light and floating icons never obscure it
- No speaking, no singing, no lip-sync, no mouthing words — a smile and a single wave are the only silent reactions
- The "LIVE" badge and viewer count appear only from the midpoint onward and must remain visible until the last frame once shown
- The floating hearts/gift icons must increase gradually in density, never appear all at once
- The phone on the tripod stays out of focus and secondary — it is never the visual subject
- No jump cuts — the entire sequence plays as one continuous, unhurried take
- No other people in frame
- No literal TikTok logo, wordmark, or app-screenshot recreation — the LIVE badge and icons must read as a generic, original streaming-UI style
