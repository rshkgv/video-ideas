Радужная грива

Визуальный якорь — плавно материализующаяся радужная грива волос: прядь за прядью, волосы пользователя обретают пастельный радужный градиент (розовый→сиреневый→голубой→мятный), будто по волшебству My Little Pony. Пользователь сидит неподвижно в мягком пастельном освещении, камера статична. В финале на скуле мягко проступает светящийся символ в стиле "cutie mark" — простая пастельная звезда — и остаётся до конца кадра.

Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a soft pastel-lit studio close-up. The person faces the camera directly, medium close-up, face centered and fully visible throughout.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is locked off, completely static, for the entire video:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: The person sits calm and still, natural hair color, soft pastel light on their face.
2. FRAMES 20–70%: Strand by strand, starting from the ends and moving upward, the person's hair gradually shifts into a pastel rainbow gradient (soft pink → lavender → sky blue → mint), each strand catching a faint sparkle as it transforms. The transformation is gradual and continuous, never an instant cut.
3. FRAMES 70–90%: The full head of hair now flows in the complete pastel rainbow gradient, gently catching the light. A small cluster of soft sparkle particles drifts down past the person's cheek.
4. FRAMES 90–100%: A small, glowing pastel star-shaped symbol softly fades in on the person's cheek or shoulder, like a magical mark settling into place, and remains glowing gently. The person's expression shifts into a warm, pleased smile — no words.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, facial features, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their face. Only the hair color/gradient changes — this is an allowed styling transformation, not an identity change. The person's face remains centered, forward-facing, and the clear visual focus for the entire video.

Key visual events (in order):
- The natural "before" baseline
- Hair transforming strand by strand into the pastel rainbow gradient (the hero moment)
- Sparkle particles drifting past the cheek
- The glowing star-shaped mark settling into place, and the person's warm smile

Style:
Soft, pastel, dreamlike fantasy aesthetic — gentle sparkle particle effects, warm diffused lighting. Wholesome and magical, not garish or neon.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's face, facial features, skin tone, or identity at any point — only the hair color/gradient may change
- The person's face must remain centered, fully visible, and the clear visual focus throughout
- No speaking, no singing, no lip-sync, no mouthing words — a warm smile is the only silent reaction
- No speech bubbles
- Do not render any copyrighted pony character, franchise logo, or wordmark of any kind — only a generic pastel star-shaped glowing symbol, original in design
- The hair transformation must be gradual and continuous, strand by strand — no instant jump cut or morph
- No jump cuts other than the natural progression described
- No other people in frame
- No social-media UI overlays
