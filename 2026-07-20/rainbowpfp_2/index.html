#rainbowpfp

Кольцо собирается из частиц

Визуальный якорь — тот же радужный тренд, но другой приём: вместо мгновенного щелчка, крошечные светящиеся частицы всех цветов радуги медленно слетаются из-за краёв кадра и постепенно выстраиваются в сплошное кольцо вокруг лица пользователя. Пользователь спокойно сидит, ощущая, как частицы кружатся вокруг него, и в момент, когда кольцо полностью формируется, закрывает глаза на секунду от удовольствия, затем открывает и смотрит в камеру.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a clean, neutral studio close-up. The person faces the camera directly, medium close-up, face centered and fully visible throughout.

Camera and motion structure:
A single slow, continuous push-in for the entire video — no cuts, no whip:
1. FRAMES 0–25%: Tiny points of rainbow-colored light drift in from the edges of frame, sparse and scattered, barely noticeable. The person sits calm and relaxed, aware of the light beginning to gather around them.
2. FRAMES 25–65%: The particles multiply and converge, tracing an increasingly solid ring around the person's face and shoulders, cycling smoothly through the rainbow gradient as they settle into place. The camera continues its slow push-in throughout.
3. FRAMES 65–85%: The ring completes, fully solid and glowing, seamlessly framing the person. They close their eyes for a moment, savoring it.
4. FRAMES 85–100%: The person opens their eyes and looks directly at the camera with a soft, satisfied smile, the completed ring glowing steadily around them. Hold to the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance. The person's face remains centered, forward-facing, and the clear visual focus for the entire video.

Key visual events (in order):
- The scattered particles drifting in from the edges
- The particles converging into a solid rainbow ring (the hero moment), tracked by the slow push-in
- The eyes-closed moment of quiet enjoyment
- The eyes opening to a soft, satisfied smile at camera

Style:
Dreamy, atmospheric studio aesthetic — the particle-to-ring formation should feel magical but precise, like a living gradient assembling itself. Calm, satisfying pacing rather than an explosive reveal.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's face, hair, skin tone, or identity at any point
- The person's face must remain centered, fully visible, and the clear visual focus throughout — the particles and ring frame the face, never obscure it
- No speaking, no singing, no lip-sync, no mouthing words — the eyes-closed pause and soft smile are the only silent reactions
- No speech bubbles
- The camera performs one continuous slow push-in only — no whip cuts, no zoom bursts, no handheld motion
- The particles must converge smoothly and gradually — no sudden snapping or jump cuts
- The rainbow gradient must remain smooth and continuous, cycling in color order, never flashing or repeating out of sequence
- No other people in frame
- No text captions or overlay graphics beyond the particle/ring effect itself
- No social-media UI overlays
