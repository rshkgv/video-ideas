#rainbowpfp

Фото окрашивается радугой

Визуальный якорь — не рамка, а сама заливка всего кадра: обычное фото пользователя целиком перекрашивается в непрерывный радужный цветовой градиент (красный→оранжевый→жёлтый→зелёный→синий→фиолетовый), как настоящий тренд #rainbowpfp, где перекрашивается вся аватарка целиком, а не только её край. Свет медленно наплывает слева направо через весь кадр, окрашивая кожу, одежду и фон в радужный дуотон одновременно. Пользователь стоит неподвижно, спокойно наблюдая, как цвет заливает кадр, и в момент завершения слегка прищуривается от удовольствия результата.

Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a plain, naturally lit medium close-up. The person faces the camera directly, medium close-up, face fully visible throughout.

Camera and motion structure:
A single slow, continuous push-in for the entire video — no cuts, no whip:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: The person stands in natural color and lighting, calm and composed, camera beginning an extremely slow push-in.
2. FRAMES 20–70%: A soft wash of rainbow-gradient color (red→orange→yellow→green→blue→purple, smooth continuous duotone) sweeps across the entire frame from left to right — recoloring the person's skin tones, clothing, and the background all together into the gradient, like a filter passing over the whole image rather than a line drawn around it. The sweep is even and continuous, never patchy.
3. FRAMES 70–90%: The rainbow duotone fully fills the frame, the entire image now rendered in the smooth gradient, the person's features still clearly legible beneath the color wash.
4. FRAMES 90–100%: The person's eyes narrow slightly in a satisfied, pleased reaction to the finished look. Hold on this fully-colored frame to the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, and identity structure exactly from the source image throughout the entire video — the rainbow color wash tints the whole image but the person's facial features, proportions, and identity must remain clearly recognizable underneath the color, never dissolved or abstracted beyond recognition.

Key visual events (in order):
- The natural-color "before" baseline
- The rainbow gradient wash sweeping left to right across the entire frame, recoloring everything together (the hero moment)
- The completed full-frame rainbow duotone, features still legible
- The satisfied, pleased eye-narrowing reaction at the end

Style:
Bright, saturated, filter-like aesthetic — the entire image reads as if a duotone/gradient-map color filter has been applied across the whole photo at once, exactly like the real #rainbowpfp trend recolors an entire profile picture, not just its edge.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's facial structure, proportions, or identity at any point — only the color grading of the whole frame changes
- The person's face must remain centered, legible, and the clear visual focus throughout, even once fully recolored — never lose the face into an abstract color field
- No speaking, no singing, no lip-sync, no mouthing words — a slight satisfied eye-narrowing is the only silent reaction
- No speech bubbles
- The color wash must sweep evenly across the whole frame (skin, clothing, and background together) — do not render it as a ring, line, or border around the face; the whole image recolors, not its edge
- The rainbow gradient must remain smooth and continuous, cycling in color order, never flashing or reversing
- No jump cuts — the sweep plays as one continuous, unhurried take
- No other people in frame
- No text captions or overlay graphics
- No social-media UI overlays
