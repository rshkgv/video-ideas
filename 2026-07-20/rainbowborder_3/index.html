#rainbowborder

Рамка в рамке

Визуальный якорь — мета-приём: камера отъезжает от лица пользователя и показывает, что портрет на самом деле выведен внутри стилизованного мокапа профиля (обобщённый интерфейс, без реальных логотипов), где вокруг фото уже стоит тонкая радужная рамка как часть оформления профиля. Затем камера снова наезжает обратно на лицо, рамка на мгновение вспыхивает ярче — будто "подтверждая" выбор оформления, — и застывает. Пользователь наблюдает за этим спокойно, с лёгким довольным кивком в конце.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a clean, neutral studio close-up that reveals itself to be displayed inside a generic, unbranded profile-picture mockup frame. The person faces the camera directly, medium close-up, face centered and fully visible throughout.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera performs one continuous pull-back followed by one continuous push-in — two smooth moves total, no other cuts:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: Tight medium close-up on the person's calm, attentive face.
2. FRAMES 20–50%: The camera pulls back steadily, revealing the shot is framed inside a simple, generic circular profile-picture mockup (a plain neutral UI card, no real app logos or text) — and around the circular photo, a thin rainbow-gradient border (red→orange→yellow→green→blue→purple) is already visible as part of the mockup's frame styling.
3. FRAMES 50–75%: The camera holds briefly on this wider view, letting the rainbow-bordered profile mockup read clearly.
4. FRAMES 75–90%: The camera pushes back in smoothly toward the person's face, the rainbow border brightening once in a soft pulse as the frame tightens back to a close-up.
5. FRAMES 90–100%: Back in close-up, the person gives one small, satisfied nod directly to camera, expression calm and pleased. Hold to the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance. The person's face remains centered, forward-facing, and the clear visual focus in every frame, including during the wider mockup view.

Key visual events (in order):
- The initial tight close-up
- The pull-back revealing the generic profile mockup with its rainbow border already in place (the hero moment)
- The brief hold on the wider mockup view
- The push-in back to close-up with one soft brightening pulse of the border
- The person's satisfied nod at the end

Style:
Clean, high-key studio aesthetic with a simple, entirely generic UI mockup card as the sole graphic element — no real social-media branding, logos, or wordmarks of any kind. Calm, confident, understated.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's face, hair, skin tone, or identity at any point
- The person's face must remain clearly visible and the visual focus in every frame, including the wider mockup view
- No speaking, no singing, no lip-sync, no mouthing words — a calm nod is the only reaction
- No speech bubbles
- The mockup UI must stay entirely generic — no real app logos, wordmarks, icons, or text of any kind
- Exactly two camera moves total — the pull-back and the push-in — no additional cuts or camera motion
- The rainbow border must be already fully formed in the wider view — no drawing or growing animation
- No other people in frame
- No overlay graphics or captions beyond the generic mockup frame itself
