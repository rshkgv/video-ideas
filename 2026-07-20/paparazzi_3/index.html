#paparazzi

Невозмутимый в буре вспышек

Визуальный якорь — контраст: пользователь стоит совершенно неподвижно и невозмутимо в центре кадра, пока вокруг него безостановочно взрываются десятки фотовспышек со всех сторон, будто он в эпицентре папарацци-шторма. Никакой драмы в лице — просто спокойное, слегка скучающее выражение "звезды", для которой это обыденность. Камера статична, единственное движение — вспышки.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a dim night-time backdrop. The person stands centered, medium shot, face clearly visible throughout, completely still.

Camera and motion structure:
Keep the overall shot completely static. No zoom, no camera movement — the flashes are the only motion, and the person's stillness is the entire point:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: The person stands calm and still, neutral relaxed expression, ambient dim light.
2. FRAMES 20–100%: Camera flashes begin popping rapidly and continuously from all sides of the frame — dense, overlapping bursts of white light, increasing in frequency, implying dozens of unseen photographers. The person does not react to the chaos at all — remains perfectly still, calm, faintly bored or amused expression, occasionally blinking naturally at a flash but never flinching or moving otherwise.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance. The person's stillness is the joke — no shift in posture, no gesture, no expression change beyond natural blinking.

Key visual events (in order):
- The calm, still starting pose
- The flashes beginning and rapidly intensifying into a continuous barrage (the hero moment is the contrast itself, not a single event)
- The person's unbothered, unmoving calm throughout the chaos

Style:
High-contrast night-time paparazzi realism — the flash barrage is intense and relentless, while the person's stillness reads as deadpan, unbothered star-power. The comedy/spectacle is entirely in the contrast.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's face, hair, skin tone, or identity at any point
- The person must remain the visual focus and clearly recognizable throughout — flash bursts never fully obscure the face for more than a single frame at a time
- No speaking, no singing, no lip-sync, no mouthing words — a natural blink is the only permitted involuntary reaction
- No speech bubbles
- The camera must remain completely static throughout — no pans, no zoom, no handheld motion
- The person must not flinch, shield their eyes, gesture, or change posture at any point — stillness is mandatory throughout
- The flash bursts must look like real camera flash photography — sharp white light bursts, not glowing or colored effects
- No visible photographers or crowd — the flashes alone imply their presence
- No jump cuts — the entire video plays as one continuous, unhurried held shot
- No text captions or overlay graphics
- No social-media UI overlays
