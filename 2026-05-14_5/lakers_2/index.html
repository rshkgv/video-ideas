Один резкий, эффектный жест — и всё.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a close-up on the person holding a closed velvet ring box in both hands, chest height, against a dark neutral background.

Camera and motion structure:
A single sudden camera whip drives the entire reveal:
1. FRAMES 0–40%: Close-up, camera holds steady. The person looks down at the closed velvet box in their hands, thumbs resting on the lid, completely still except for slow breathing.
2. FRAMES 40–55%: In one motion, the person's thumbs flip the lid open. Exactly as the lid opens, the camera does a sudden, fast push-in toward the box.
3. FRAMES 55–75%: Inside, a championship ring is revealed — a large purple-and-gold gemstone cluster catching the light, throwing sharp purple-gold reflections across the person's face and hands.
4. FRAMES 75–100%: The camera holds tight on the ring's glow as the person slowly lifts their gaze from the ring to the camera, eyes shining, a small proud smile forming. Hold on this moment to the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, happening only once: stillness holding the closed box → thumbs flip the lid open → gaze lowers to the ring → gaze lifts to camera with pride. No other movement, no walking, no talking.

Key visual events (in order):
- The closed velvet box held reverently in both hands
- The sudden lid-flip synchronized exactly with the camera's push-in (the hero moment)
- The purple-gold gemstone ring catching and throwing light
- The slow, proud gaze lift to camera

Style:
Intimate, reverent, high-contrast product-reveal cinematography — think a jewelry macro shot crossed with a championship-moment close-up. Deep shadows, sharp purple-gold light reflections off the ring as the only bright accent. Emotionally weighty, not loud.

Restrictions:

- No crowd, no other people, no arena background
- No overlay graphics, no text, no captions of any kind
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media UI overlays
- No violent or aggressive actions
- No jump cuts — the lid-flip and camera push-in must be one continuous, perfectly synchronized motion
- The ring must remain the single brightest, most detailed element in every frame after it's revealed
