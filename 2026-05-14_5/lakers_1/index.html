#lakers

Ритуал в раздевалке

Визуальный якорь: раздевалка Lakers — фиолетово-золотые шкафчики с гербом команды, майка на плечиках, кроссовки на скамье. Пользователь сидит один перед своим шкафчиком в тихой предматчевой сосредоточенности: завязывает шнурки, поправляет напульсники, натягивает капюшон толстовки поверх головы. Ни толпы, ни шума — только собранность и ритуал перед выходом на паркет. Камера статична, свет холодный и функциональный, как в настоящей раздевалке НБА.

Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is an NBA locker room before a game. The person sits alone on a wooden bench in front of an open locker, medium shot, purple-and-gold team gear visible around them.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is completely locked off for the entire video — a single static frame on the bench and locker, no movement, no zoom:
1. FRAMES 0–30%: The person sits still, elbows on knees, staring at the floor in quiet focus. A Lakers jersey hangs visible in the open locker behind them, team crest facing camera.
2. FRAMES 30–55%: The person bends down and methodically laces up one basketball sneaker, then the other — deliberate, unhurried movements.
3. FRAMES 55–80%: The person stands, pulls on a purple-and-gold hoodie, and adjusts a wristband on each wrist — gearing up piece by piece.
4. FRAMES 80–100%: The person pulls the hoodie up over their head, casting their face in partial shadow, and holds still — a final moment of stillness before stepping out. Hold on this quiet, focused pose to the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, happening only once: sitting in stillness → lacing sneakers → putting on hoodie and wristbands → pulling hood up. No other movement, no walking out of frame, no talking.

Key visual events (in order):
- The Lakers jersey and crest visible in the open locker throughout, grounding the setting
- The deliberate, ritualistic lacing of the sneakers
- Gearing up in team purple-and-gold, piece by piece (the hero moment)
- The hood pulled up — quiet intensity, final beat

Style:
Quiet, disciplined, pre-game realism — closer to a behind-the-scenes NBA documentary than a broadcast celebration. Cool, functional locker-room lighting, muted purple-gold tones. No music-video energy — focus and readiness are the whole point.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, or identity at any point
- The camera must remain completely static throughout — no pans, no zoom, no handheld motion
- No crowd, no other players, no other people anywhere in the locker room
- No overlay graphics, no text, no captions of any kind
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media UI overlays
- No violent or aggressive actions
- No jump cuts — the entire ritual plays as one continuous, unhurried sequence
- The Lakers jersey and crest must remain clearly visible in the locker throughout frames 0–80%
