В студии подкаста с Кевином Хартом

Визуальный якорь — студия подкаста: акустические поролоновые панели на стенах, два микрофона на журавлях, крупные студийные наушники, светящаяся вывеска «ON AIR» сбоку. Теперь в кадре реально виден сам Кевин Харт — сидит за соседним микрофоном, оживлённо жестикулирует и рассказывает историю (его слова видны в стилизованном speech bubble рядом с ним). Пользователь сидит ближе к камере, в фокусе кадра, молча слушает — и сам не выдерживает, срывается в заразительный хохот. Пользователь никогда не говорит и не поёт — только слушает и реагирует эмоцией; вся речь принадлежит Кевину Харту.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is completely static throughout — a locked podcast-studio medium shot favoring the person in the foreground:
1. FRAMES 0–30%: Kevin Hart leans into his mic, animated hand gestures, visibly talking mid-story — a small stylized speech bubble beside him briefly shows a few words of what he's saying. The person in the foreground listens, mouth closed, a growing amused smile building in their eyes.
2. FRAMES 30–60%: Kevin Hart's story reaches its punchline, gesturing bigger, clearly delivering the line. The person in the foreground can't hold it together — a grin breaks first, then a genuine, unforced laugh takes over, head tipping back slightly, hand slapping the desk once. The person's mouth opens only in laughter, never shaping words.
3. FRAMES 60–85%: The person's laugh peaks — doubled slightly forward over the desk, headphones slipping a little off one ear from the motion, still laughing. Kevin Hart, still visible in the background, laughs along at his own joke. The "ON AIR" sign flickers once, brighter, in sync with the person's laugh peak.
4. FRAMES 85–100%: The person's laughter settles into a wide, satisfied grin, leaning back in the chair. Kevin Hart, still in frame, gives a satisfied nod. Hold on this warm, unforced moment to the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance. The person stays the largest, closest, most in-focus figure in every frame.
Motion arc for the person, happening only once: listening with building amusement → punchline breaks composure → full unforced laughter → settled satisfied grin. No walking, no standing, no other movement. The person is never the source of any dialogue — only Kevin Hart speaks.

Key visual events (in order):
- Kevin Hart visibly talking and gesturing, speech bubble briefly showing his line
- The person's building amusement while listening, mouth closed
- The genuine laugh breaking through — the hero moment
- The "ON AIR" sign flickering once in sync with the laugh's peak
- Kevin Hart's satisfied nod and the person's settled grin at the end

Style:
Warm, intimate podcast-studio realism — soft key light, visible acoustic foam texture, the red "ON AIR" glow as the single accent color against neutral studio tones. Unpolished and genuine, like a real recorded moment rather than a staged bit.

Restrictions:
