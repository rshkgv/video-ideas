#youngsheldon_1

Смешок за кадром

Пользователь превращается в стилизованного мультяшного персонажа в духе ретро-ситкома CBS конца 80-х: тёплая, слегка зернистая картинка, лёгкая виньетка «студийного зала». Один короткий дедпан-жест — палец в камеру и фирменная сериальная полуулыбка «я только что сказал что-то гениальное». В момент жеста раздаётся стилизованный смешок закадровой аудитории и загорается значок «DING ✨». Идеальный луп для фан-эдитов цитат из сериала.

Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a stylized 3D-cartoon rendering of a warm 1980s sitcom kitchen set. The character sits at the table in a medium close-up, deadpan expression, finger already raised.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera remains completely still throughout — a locked single-camera sitcom shot. No zoom, no pan, no movement.
1. FRAMES 0–30%: The character holds a still, deadpan pose — finger raised, chin slightly lifted, eyes fixed on camera. A beat of held silence.
2. FRAMES 30–55%: The character delivers one small, sharp jerk of the raised finger toward the camera — the "iconic one-liner" punctuation gesture — paired with the smallest widening of the eyes for comic emphasis.
3. FRAMES 55–75%: Immediately on the gesture landing, a warm golden "DING ✨" icon flashes once in the upper corner and a soft studio-audience laughter ripple (visualized only as a gentle warm light pulse across the vignette edges, no depicted crowd) washes across the frame.
4. FRAMES 75–100%: The character holds the same deadpan half-smile, completely still, as the vignette glow settles into a steady warm pulse — built to loop seamlessly back to frame 0.

Character:
Preserve the character's face, hair, and identity exactly as established from the source image throughout the entire video, in the same stylized 3D-cartoon rendering style. Do not alter their proportions or facial structure.
The only movement is: the single finger-point gesture and the small eye-widen. No other body motion, no walking, no scene change.

Key visual events (in order):
- Held deadpan pose (setup)
- One sharp finger-point gesture (the hero moment — the "punchline beat")
- "DING ✨" icon flash + warm light-pulse laughter cue
- Return to stillness, built for a seamless loop

Style:
Cute, warm, stylized 3D-cartoon sitcom look — soft rounded character shapes, warm amber studio lighting, gentle film-grain vignette suggesting a live audience just out of frame. Comedic timing is entirely in the stillness-then-snap-gesture contrast. Non-photorealistic, animated-short aesthetic throughout.

Restrictions:
- Do not duplicate the character or add any new characters — only the person from the original photo appears
- Do not switch to a photorealistic style at any point — the entire video stays in the stylized 3D-cartoon rendering
- The camera must remain completely static — no zoom, no pan, no cuts
- The "DING ✨" icon appears only once, in the final 45% of the video, and must remain visible until the last frame
- No depicted audience members, silhouettes, or crowd — the "laughter" is conveyed only through the light-pulse vignette effect, never through rendered people
- No speech bubbles, captions, or dialogue text
- No social-media UI overlays
- No fast or jittery motion — the single gesture must read as one clean, deliberate snap
