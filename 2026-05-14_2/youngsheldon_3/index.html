#youngsheldon_3

Семейный ассистент

Тёплая кухня в стиле восточно-техасской семьи конца 80-х. На столе разложен самодельный проект для научной ярмарки — картонный макет, детали, провода. Семейный пёс с абсолютно серьёзным видом носом и лапой подталкивает детали на нужные места, как будто и правда «помогает» с экспериментом. Пользователь сидит рядом, умилённо наблюдает, ничего не трогает и не мешает — только тихая улыбка гордости. Пёс продолжает деловито «работать» до самого конца. В финале — тёплый титр в стиле CBS-титров: «East Texas Tech Science Fair · Помощник дня».

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a warm East Texas family kitchen, late 1980s. A homemade science-fair project sits on the table — a cardboard model with small parts and wires. The family dog stands at the table edge, nose-nudging a piece into place. The person from the image sits beside the table, watching.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is completely static throughout — a locked medium shot.
1. FRAMES 0–25%: The dog is actively nose-nudging and paw-tapping a small part of the project into position with earnest, focused determination — repetitive, confident, task-focused motions. The person sits calmly beside the table, watching with a warm, amused expression, hands in their lap.
2. FRAMES 25–55%: The dog continues, now carefully pushing a second piece with its nose toward the model, small balancing shifts of its front paws on the table edge, ears perked in concentration. The person's only movement is a soft, fond head-tilt and a widening smile — pure quiet pride, no interference.
3. FRAMES 55–80%: The dog gives one final confident nose-nudge that settles the last piece into place, then sits back on its haunches looking satisfied. The person gives one small, delighted laugh — a slight shoulder shake, no words.
4. FRAMES 80–100%: The camera holds. A CBS-style warm title card fades in as a screen-locked overlay: "East Texas Tech Science Fair" with a smaller line "Helper of the Day 🐾" underneath, clean period serif font. Stays until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
The person's motion is minimal and realistic throughout: a soft head-tilt, a widening smile, one small delighted laugh-shake. The person does not touch the project and does not help the dog at any point.

Animal note: the dog's paws and nose must remain clearly and anatomically correct throughout — real paw and snout shapes, physically plausible nudging and balancing motions, no human-like hand gestures.

Key visual events (in order):
- Dog nose/paw-nudging the first project piece into place (task-focused, earnest)
- Dog nudging the second piece while the person watches proudly (the emotional core)
- Final piece settles — dog sits back satisfied (the hero moment)
- Warm "Helper of the Day" title card locking in

Style:
Warm, wholesome, nostalgic single-camera sitcom look — soft ambers, gentle film grain, cozy kitchen lighting. The comedy and warmth come entirely from the dog's earnest seriousness contrasted with the person's calm, proud stillness.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's face, hair, skin tone, or identity at any point
- The dog's paws and nose must remain clearly and anatomically correct animal anatomy throughout — no human-like hands or gestures
- The person does not help the dog and does not touch the project at any point
- The "Helper of the Day" title card appears only in the final 20% and must remain visible until the last frame
- No other people in frame
- No modern elements — nothing post-1989
- No speech bubbles or captions other than the specified title card
- No social-media UI overlays
- No aggressive or violent actions
