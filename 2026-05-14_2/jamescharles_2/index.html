#jamescharles_2

Распаковка палетки

Пользователь держит в руках закрытую палетку теней и смотрит в камеру с интригующей улыбкой — "распаковка" в духе бьюти-коллабораций Джеймса Чарльза. Резкий наезд камеры на палетку в момент открытия — и мгновенный отъезд назад раскрывает пользователя уже полностью преображённым: сияющий макияж, профессиональный визажный свет, вращающееся зеркало со светодиодной подсветкой на фоне. Один хлопок — и готовый образ. В финале вспыхивает мерцающий графический росчерк.

Turn the photo of the person into a medium close-up shot of them holding a closed, sleek eyeshadow palette up near their face with both hands, looking directly at camera with an anticipatory half-smile, soft neutral studio background, natural everyday makeup.
Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene opens as a medium close-up: the person holds a closed eyeshadow palette near their face, looking at camera with anticipation.

Camera and motion structure:
1. FRAMES 0–25%: Medium close-up, static hold. The person looks at camera, thumbs on the palette's clasp, building anticipation with a small knowing smile.
2. FRAMES 25–45%: Sudden, fast push-in toward the palette as it clicks open — the motion is quick and decisive, almost a snap-zoom, landing tight on the opening palette and the person's hands.
3. FRAMES 45–55%: A bright flash-cut of light (a lens-flare bloom, not a scene cut) fills the frame for an instant as the "reveal" happens off-camera.
4. FRAMES 55–85%: Sudden, equally fast pull-back reveals the person now fully transformed — flawless full-glam makeup already complete (bold blended eyeshadow, sharp winged liner, glowing highlighter, glossy lips), seated at a professional vanity setup with a ring light and warm bulb-framed mirror glowing behind them. Their pose is confident, chin lifted, straight into camera.
5. FRAMES 85–100%: The shot holds on the glam reveal. A shimmering graphic flourish animates in around the frame edges — thin gold sparkle lines tracing inward toward the corners, then settling still. The look remains fully visible until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout. Do not alter facial structure, bone structure, or identity.
Allowed: the instant appearance of full makeup and a change of immediate setting (vanity/ring-light backdrop) at the pull-back — this is the intended "glow-up cut," not a gradual application.
Forbidden: any change to eye shape, nose, or lip shape; any change to the person's actual facial identity.

Main action:
The hero moment is the snap push-in/pull-back pair at FRAMES 25–85% — the palette opening and the instant full-glam reveal must feel like one seamless, fast, satisfying cut-on-motion, not two separate shots.

Style:
Glossy influencer glow-up energy — think a James Charles-style "get ready in one click" collab-palette bit. Bright, punchy studio lighting on the reveal; warm-neutral, anticipatory lighting before it. Bold but tasteful full-glam makeup — professional, camera-ready.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's facial structure, eye shape, nose, or lip shape at any point — only makeup and immediate surroundings change
- The reveal must happen through the single push-in/flash/pull-back motion — no jump cuts, no fade transitions
- The shimmer graphic flourish appears only in the final 15% and must remain visible until the last frame
- No text captions or social-media UI overlays
- No extra people in frame
- No aggressive or fast body movements beyond the camera's own push-in/pull-back
- The palette itself must remain visible in the person's hand or on the vanity after the reveal — it should not vanish
