#lakers_2

Селфи с корта

Пользователь снимает себя на телефон с первого ряда во время матча Lakers — рука вытянута, камера слегка трясётся от шума трибун. Внезапно игра приближается вплотную к камере: мяч пролетает у самого объектива, силуэты игроков смазанным пятном проносятся на первом плане. Пользователь не пугается — на лице восторг и адреналин. Кадр держится от первого лица до самого конца, а в финале — торжествующий кулак вверх у самой камеры.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is the front row of a Lakers game, shot entirely as a handheld arm's-length selfie video. The person from the image holds the camera themselves, medium close-up, court action visible just behind them.

Camera and motion structure:
The entire video is a continuous handheld POV shot — natural arm-length shake and micro-adjustments throughout, no cuts:
1. FRAMES 0–25%: The person grins at the camera, courtside noise and purple-and-gold blur in the background. Arena lights flare occasionally. Natural handheld wobble.
2. FRAMES 25–55%: Their eyes widen — something is happening on court just out of frame. The hand holding the phone instinctively tilts slightly toward the action, still keeping their own face mostly in frame.
3. FRAMES 55–80%: The play comes right at them — a basketball whips past close to the lens (motion-blurred, not making contact), and blurred player silhouettes streak across the extreme foreground for an instant. The person flinches back for a split second, then breaks into a huge excited laugh.
4. FRAMES 80–100%: The person pulls the phone back to a clean selfie framing, pumping one fist up right next to the lens in triumph, mouth open in a joyful shout. Hold on this close, celebratory frame to the end.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Character arc: grinning excitement → wide-eyed anticipation → startled flinch-then-laugh → triumphant fist pump. The person's expression is thrilled and amazed throughout, never afraid.

Key visual events (in order):
- The basketball whipping past close to the lens with motion blur (the hero moment)
- Blurred player silhouettes streaking through the extreme foreground
- The flinch-into-laughter beat
- The fist-pump-to-camera finish

Style:
Realistic courtside phone-camera footage — handheld shake, occasional lens flare from arena floodlights, slight motion blur on fast action, natural audio-reactive energy implied through the person's expressions. Raw, immediate, thrilling — like a viral courtside clip.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- The basketball and any player silhouettes must never actually contact the person or camera — near-miss only
- The person's face must remain the clear visual focus throughout — court action stays secondary/background/foreground-blur
- No slow motion
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media overlays (no like/comment counters)
- No text captions of any kind
- No extra rendered faces in sharp focus other than the person from the reference photo
- No violent or aggressive collisions
- Camera must remain a continuous handheld shot — no cuts, no scene resets
