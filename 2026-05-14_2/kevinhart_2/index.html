#kevinhart_2

Единственный с каменным лицом

Комната взрывается смехом от шутки комика (подразумевается Кевин Харт, слышен только за кадром) — все вокруг тебя падают от хохота, вытирают слёзы, хватаются за животы. Ты в центре кадра держишь абсолютно каменное лицо, ни один мускул не дрогнет. Камера полностью статична. Чем безумнее реагирует зал вокруг, тем нелепее выглядит твоя стойкость — пока в конце экран не подписывает шутку плашкой «UNBOTHERED».

Turn the photo of the person into a medium shot of them standing dead-center in a crowded living-room watch party, deadpan neutral expression, arms crossed, surrounded by out-of-focus people already mid-laugh, a TV glow visible at the frame edge.
Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a crowded watch-party room, everyone reacting to a comedian's joke (implied Kevin Hart, heard only as an off-screen voice/TV audio, never shown on camera). The person from the image stands dead-center, completely composed.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is absolutely locked off for the entire video — no movement, no zoom, no pan:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: The person stands center-frame, arms crossed, totally deadpan neutral expression. Around them, other people are just beginning to react to a punchline — first small chuckles.
2. FRAMES 20–55%: The people around the person escalate into full laughter — doubling over, slapping their knees, wiping their eyes, leaning on each other for support. The person in the center does not move a single muscle: no blink change, no smile, no shift in posture.
3. FRAMES 55–85%: The laughter around the person peaks into chaos — people nearly falling off furniture, gasping for air. The person remains utterly still, arms still crossed, deadpan, maybe one single slow blink and nothing else.
4. FRAMES 85–100%: Camera holds on the frozen contrast. An overlay graphic fades in as a screen-locked element:
   — Bottom-center: "UNBOTHERED 😐" in bold clean sans-serif
   It remains until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
The person in the center never moves, blinks more than once, gestures, or changes expression — this is the entire joke.

Key visual events (in order):
- Background people escalating from chuckles to full hysterical laughter
- The center person's total stillness in contrast — the hero moment IS the absence of reaction
- Overlay graphic locking in during the final 15%

Style:
Deadpan comedic realism, flat naturalistic lighting like a real living room, handheld-adjacent framing but the camera itself never moves. The comedy is purely visual contrast — chaos versus stillness.

Restrictions:
- The center person never moves, blinks (beyond one slow blink), gestures, or changes expression
- All background people remain visually distinct at all times — they never merge, overlap, duplicate, disappear, or change identity
- Do not add or remove any people — only animate the people already present in the original image, plus generic background reactions if the frame requires filling out a crowd
- Camera remains absolutely locked off — no movement, no zoom, no pan
- The "UNBOTHERED 😐" graphic appears only in the final 15% and must remain visible until the last frame
- No speech bubbles or captions quoting jokes
- No social-media UI overlays
- No text other than the specified overlay
- No aggressive or distressed reactions from the background people — only genuine, joyful laughter
