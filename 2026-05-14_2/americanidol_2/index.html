#americanidol

Кастинг-видео из спальни

Пользователь снимает себя на телефон в своей комнате для домашнего видео-кастинга #americanidol — держит телефон на вытянутой руке, немного нервничает, поправляет волосы, делает вдох. Перед тем как запеть, он на секунду замирает и смотрит прямо в камеру — лицо крупно и чётко видно. Затем звучит уверенная, мощная нота, и в этот момент свет вокруг него мягко меняется на тёплый студийный, будто он уже не дома, а его «заметили». В финале — нижняя плашка «YOU'VE BEEN DISCOVERED ✦».

Turn the photo of the person into a slightly wide-angle phone-selfie shot of them sitting on the edge of a bed in a cozy bedroom, holding a phone at arm's length toward the camera, natural warm lamp light, a nervous half-smile, hair being touched up with one hand, casual homewear.
Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a handheld phone-selfie "audition tape" recorded at home for the #americanidol hashtag challenge. The person from the image holds the phone at arm's length, slight wide-angle lens distortion typical of a front camera, natural bedroom lighting.

Camera and motion structure:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: Handheld selfie framing, small natural phone shake. The person looks at the camera, adjusts their hair with one hand, exhales visibly — a nervous pre-performance beat. Face clearly visible and expressive throughout this pause.
2. FRAMES 20–40%: They take one visible breath, shoulders settle, expression shifts from nervous to focused and determined. The phone holds steady in their hand.
3. FRAMES 40–70%: They open into a confident, powerful sung note — head lifts slightly, eyes closing briefly then opening with conviction. As the note builds, the warm bedroom lamp light gradually shifts and intensifies into a cooler, brighter studio-quality glow, as if the room itself is transforming around the performance.
4. FRAMES 70–100%: The note holds, full of confidence. The handheld shake settles into a near-still frame. A lower-third overlay fades in: "YOU'VE BEEN DISCOVERED ✦" in a clean glowing sans-serif, with a small red starburst American Idol logo in the corner. Both remain until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc: nervous fidget → steadying breath → confident sung note. The phone-holding arm stays in frame throughout, consistent with a real selfie recording.

Key visual events (in order):
- The nervous pre-action beat (hair touch, breath) — establishes this is a real, vulnerable self-tape
- The lighting transformation from warm bedroom lamp to studio-cool glow, synced to the note building (the hero moment)
- The "YOU'VE BEEN DISCOVERED ✦" overlay locking in at the end

Style:
Authentic phone-selfie realism transitioning into polished broadcast glow — the contrast between "recorded at home" and "suddenly professional" is the whole point. Handheld, intimate, slightly imperfect at the start; clean and cinematic by the end.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- The phone-holding arm and selfie framing must remain visible and consistent throughout — no camera cuts to a third-person view
- The "YOU'VE BEEN DISCOVERED ✦" overlay and logo appear only in the final quarter and must remain fully visible until the last frame
- No lip-sync errors — mouth movement must match a single sustained note, not words
- No social-media UI overlays (no likes, comments, follower counts)
- No text captions other than the specified overlay
- No extra people in frame
- No aggressive or violent actions
- The lighting shift must be gradual and continuous, not a jump cut
