Ночь легенд

Тёмная притихшая арена после матча — прожектор освещает центр площадки, в полумраке под потолком мягко светятся баннеры с номерами легенд Lakers. Пользователь стоит один в фиолетово-золотой майке, рука на груди, взгляд в камеру — почти без движения, только медленное дыхание. Это не праздник, а момент благоговения перед историей клуба. В финале — тихий, элегантный титр «LEGACY NIGHT» и мягкая вспышка золотого света на форме.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a quiet, emptied-out NBA arena after hours — a single spotlight on center court. The person from the image stands alone in a Lakers jersey, medium shot, hand over heart.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is almost entirely still — this is a moment of reverence, not spectacle:
1. FRAMES 0–35%: Medium shot, spotlight steady on the person. The arena around them is dark and silent; faint glowing jersey banners are visible high in the rafters. The person stands still, hand over heart, eyes closed.
2. FRAMES 35–65%: An extremely slow, barely perceptible push-in begins. The person takes one slow, deep breath — chest rising and falling once. Their eyes open, gaze lifting toward the rafters.
3. FRAMES 65–90%: The person lowers their gaze back to the camera. A single soft golden light sweep washes over them from above, catching the fabric of the jersey. Their expression is calm, proud, moved.
4. FRAMES 90–100%: The shot holds. A minimal, elegant title fades in at the bottom: "LEGACY NIGHT" in a thin gold serif, with a small laurel-wreath icon beside it. It remains until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
The only permitted motion: one slow breath, eyes opening and lifting then returning to camera, and stillness otherwise. No walking, no arm movement, no cheering.

Key visual events (in order):
- Faint retired-jersey banners glowing softly in the dark rafters (background only, no readable player names)
- The single slow breath and gaze lift — the emotional core of the video
- The golden light sweep across the jersey fabric
- The "LEGACY NIGHT" title fading in at the very end

Style:
Quiet, reverent, cinematic — closer to a memorial montage than a broadcast celebration. Deep near-black arena tones with a single warm spotlight; the gold light sweep is the only "spectacle" moment. Understated, emotionally heavy, dignified.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- Do not depict any specific real athlete's face or likeness anywhere in the frame — the rafter banners must show only glowing shapes/numbers, no readable names and no rendered human figures
- The "LEGACY NIGHT" title appears only in the final 10% and must remain visible until the last frame
- No confetti, no crowd, no cheering, no fast movement
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media overlays
- No text captions other than the "LEGACY NIGHT" title
- Camera motion must stay to an extremely slow, barely-visible push-in — no pans, no zoom bursts
- No other people visible in the arena
