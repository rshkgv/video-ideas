За кулисами после номера

Сразу после выступления в финале American Idol пользователь стоит за кулисами, ещё не отдышавшись, — эмоции берут верх. Слёзы облегчения и радости, тихая улыбка. Рядом, частично в кадре, — фигура наставника/продюсера, которая молча приобнимает его за плечо. Камера полностью статична, почти нет движения — только дыхание, моргание, одна слеза. В воздухе мягко парят тёплые частицы света со сцены.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is backstage at the American Idol finale, moments after the person's performance. The person from the image stands close to camera, medium close-up, still catching their breath.

Camera and motion structure:
Keep the overall shot static. No zoom, no camera movement — a locked-off intimate close-up.
1. FRAMES 0–25%: The person's chest rises and falls, breath still uneven from performing. Eyes are glassy, unfocused, processing what just happened.
2. FRAMES 25–55%: A single tear escapes and slides down one cheek. Their gaze softens, lips part slightly in a shaky exhale.
3. FRAMES 55–80%: A hand — belonging to an implied mentor or producer figure, only partially visible at the edge of frame — rests gently on their shoulder. The person's eyes close briefly at the touch, a small grateful smile forming.
4. FRAMES 80–100%: The person opens their eyes, looks toward camera, and lets a warm, relieved smile fully form. Soft warm particles of stage light drift gently across the frame throughout this section, thicker toward the end, like glowing dust caught in a spotlight beam. No overlay graphics — the moment stays intimate and unadorned.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc: uneven breathing → a single tear → a shoulder touch and closed eyes → a warm, open smile. No other body movement — no walking, no gesture beyond what is described.

Key visual events (in order):
- The single tear (establishes vulnerability)
- The partially-visible hand on the shoulder (the human connection, the quiet hero moment)
- The final warm smile directly to camera

Style:
Intimate, warm, unglamorous in the best way — the opposite of the bombastic stage moment. Soft amber and gold tones, shallow depth of field, backstage authenticity. No music-video polish; this should feel like a stolen, real moment.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- No speaking, no lip-sync, no dialogue — only breath, a tear, and a smile
- The implied mentor/producer figure must remain partially out of frame or out of focus — never a fully depicted second face
- No text captions, no overlay graphics, no social-media UI of any kind
- No camera movement of any kind — the shot remains completely locked off
- No extra people beyond the one partially-visible hand/shoulder
- No aggressive, distressed, or negative expressions — this is relief and joy only
