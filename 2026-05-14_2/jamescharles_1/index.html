#jamescharles

Глянец крупным планом

Экстремальный крупный план на губах пользователя — ASMR-бьюти в духе туториалов Джеймса Чарльза. Невидимый аппликатор наносит один плавный взмах прозрачного блеска, глянец растекается и ловит свет, на нижнюю губу оседает лёгкая россыпь мельчайших блёсток. В финале — губы смыкаются с лёгким блеском, глаза на миг закрываются от удовольствия. Никакой спешки — только текстура, свет и один идеальный жест.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is an extreme close-up beauty shot, cropped tightly to the person's lower face and lips, soft ring-light studio lighting.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is completely static throughout — a locked macro-style beauty close-up:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: Lips bare, slightly parted, catching soft light. No motion except a faint natural breath.
2. FRAMES 20–55%: An invisible applicator sweeps once across the lower lip, left to right — a clear glossy product spreading in one continuous, unhurried stroke. The gloss catches the light as it spreads, developing a wet-look sheen.
3. FRAMES 55–80%: Fine glitter particles drift down and settle onto the freshly glossed lower lip, catching micro-glints of light one by one. The gloss sheen deepens and pools slightly at the center of the lip.
4. FRAMES 80–100%: The lips press together once, sealing the gloss evenly. A single slow, satisfied eye-adjacent muscle relaxation reads as pleasure (the eyes, if visible at the edge of frame, soften and half-close for a beat). The shot holds on the glossy, glitter-flecked lips.

Character:
Preserve the person's lips, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout. Do not alter lip shape or facial structure.
Allowed: gloss product appearing and spreading, glitter settling, a soft close-of-eyes pleasure beat. Forbidden: any change to lip shape, teeth, or skin texture beyond the gloss/glitter itself.

Main action:
The single hero moment is the glitter settling into the wet gloss sheen at FRAMES 55–80% — every fleck should catch a distinct micro-glint as it lands.

Style:
Elevated ASMR beauty macro shot — think James Charles's satisfying gloss-application close-up content. Soft pastel-pink blur behind, warm even studio light, tactile realism: the gloss must look wet and viscous, not painted-on flat color.

Restrictions:
- Do not change lip shape, teeth, or facial structure at any point
- No visible hand, brush, or applicator in frame — the gloss application must read as invisible/magical, only the product motion is visible
- No text overlays, captions, or graphics of any kind
- No social-media UI overlays
- No jump cuts — the gloss stroke and glitter settling must be smooth, continuous motion
- No aggressive or fast movements — the pacing stays slow and ASMR-deliberate throughout
- Background must remain the same blurred pastel studio backdrop throughout
