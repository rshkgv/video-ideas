#americanidol

Золотой билет

Визуальный якорь — судейский стол American Idol: три силуэта за подсвеченной стойкой с именными табличками, за спиной пользователя светится фирменный логотип шоу, единственный прожектор сверху, микрofон в руке. Пользователь стоит перед судьями в ожидании вердикта, камера медленно наезжает на его лицо. На резком акценте вперёд протягивается светящийся золотой билет — и в кадр взрывается золотое конфетти-ленты. Никакой сцены с толпой, никакого закулисья — именно момент вердикта перед судейским столом.

Turn the photo of the person into a medium shot standing on a spotlit audition stage, facing a raised judges' desk with three blurred/silhouetted judge figures behind glowing nameplate lighting, a large glowing red starburst "IDOL" logo backdrop behind the person, wireless microphone held at chest height, a single dramatic spotlight from above, tense hopeful expression.
Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a televised singing-competition audition stage. The person from the image stands center-stage facing a raised judges' desk — three figures visible only as soft silhouettes behind bright nameplate-style desk lighting, never rendered as identifiable individuals. A glowing red starburst "IDOL" logo backdrop looms behind the person.

Camera and motion structure:
1. FRAMES 0–40%: Medium shot. The person stands still, microphone at chest height, eyes flicking toward the judges' desk. The camera begins a slow, deliberate push-in toward their face. The glowing logo backdrop pulses faintly behind them.
2. FRAMES 40–65%: The push-in continues, tightening to a close-up. The person's breathing is visibly held, jaw tight, eyes wide with anticipation. The silhouetted judges lean forward slightly at the desk (implied motion only, never detailed faces).
3. FRAMES 65–80%: On a single sharp beat, an arm from the judges' desk extends a glowing golden ticket/paper into frame toward the person. The camera holds tight on the ticket catching the spotlight.
4. FRAMES 80–100%: The person's hand rises to take the ticket, eyes brimming, a huge smile breaking. Gold ribbon confetti bursts from above. The camera settles on a held close-up of the ticket in their hand against their chest.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, each stage happening only once: held-breath stillness → tightening tension → receiving the ticket → open joyful reaction. No other people are ever clearly depicted — the judges remain silhouettes at the desk throughout.

Key visual events (in order):
- The glowing red starburst logo backdrop, visible throughout as the show's signature visual identity
- The silhouetted judges' desk with nameplate lighting
- The golden ticket extended into frame on one sharp beat — the hero moment
- Gold ribbon confetti bursting as the person takes the ticket

Style:
Live TV talent-show drama — warm spotlight, deep black stage surroundings, the glowing logo backdrop as the dominant color accent. Cinematic broadcast tension building to a bright, triumphant payoff.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- The judges must remain soft-focus silhouettes behind their desk lighting at all times — never clearly detailed faces or identifiable individuals
- The glowing "IDOL" logo backdrop must remain visible behind the person for the entire video
- No additional text, captions, or vote counts
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media UI overlays
- Gold ribbon confetti only — no fire, no explosive pyrotechnics
- No jump cuts — the entire sequence is one continuous push-in with a single sharp cut-in on the ticket extension
- The microphone remains in the person's other hand throughout
