Гость ток-шоу

Визуальный якорь — вечернее ток-шоу: гостевой диван, рядом угадывается ведущий за своим столом (виден только силуэтом), за спиной — светящийся графический задник с силуэтом ночного города, типичный для эфира вечернего шоу. Пользователь сидит на диване гостя, реагирует на реплику ведущего искренним, неудержимым смехом — та самая непринуждённая харизма, с которой звёзды вроде Кевина Харта держатся в эфире таких передач. Камера статична, ничего не происходит кроме одного настоящего смешного момента. Никакой сцены, никакого ростера — просто уютный вечерний эфир.

The scene is a late-night talk-show set. The person sits on the guest couch, a host's desk visible at the frame edge (host implied only as a silhouette, never a detailed face), a glowing city-skyline backdrop behind them.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is completely static throughout — a locked talk-show-couch medium shot:
1. FRAMES 0–30%: The person sits relaxed on the couch, listening to an implied off-screen remark from the host's silhouette at the frame edge, a small amused smile building.
2. FRAMES 30–60%: The remark lands and the person's composure breaks into a genuine laugh — head tipping back against the couch, hand slapping their own knee once, shoulders shaking.
3. FRAMES 60–85%: The laugh peaks — person leaning forward slightly, wiping the corner of one eye, still catching their breath from laughing. The city-skyline backdrop glows steadily behind them throughout, unchanging.
4. FRAMES 85–100%: The laughter settles into a wide grin, person leaning back into the couch, shaking their head slightly at the joke. Hold on this relaxed, warm moment to the last frame — no overlay graphics, the talk-show set itself is the visual signature.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
Motion arc, happening only once: relaxed listening → composure breaking into laughter → laughter peaking → settled grin. The person stays seated on the couch throughout — no standing, no walking.

Key visual events (in order):
- The amused smile building as the host's silhouette speaks off-screen
- The genuine laugh breaking through — the hero moment
- The glowing city-skyline backdrop, steady and unchanging, anchoring the talk-show setting throughout
- The settled, relaxed grin at the end

Style:
Warm, glossy late-night-broadcast realism — soft key light on the couch, the cool glow of the skyline backdrop as a color contrast, a relaxed and intimate one-on-one conversational feel rather than a stage performance.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- The host must remain a silhouette or out-of-focus figure at the frame edge — never rendered as a clearly detailed, recognizable face
- Do not depict any real, identifiable celebrity as a separate character anywhere in the frame
- The camera must remain completely static throughout — no pans, no zoom, no handheld motion
- No overlay graphics, no text, no captions of any kind
- No speech bubbles or captions quoting the joke
- No social-media UI overlays
- No jump cuts — the entire moment plays as one continuous, unhurried take
- No aggressive, mean-spirited, or distressed expressions — genuine, warm laughter only
