#rainbowpfptrend

Радужный контур силуэта

Визуальный якорь — не заливка всего кадра и не рамка вокруг фото, а тонкий радужный светящийся контур, обводящий силуэт пользователя, как неоновый рим-лайт. Пользователь сидит неподвижно, обычный цвет кожи и фона остаются нетронутыми — только по краю силуэта (плечи, линия волос, профиль) снизу вверх постепенно проступает и разгорается тонкая радужная светящаяся кромка. Камера статична, единственное движение — растущий по контуру свет, в финале пользователь слегка улыбается, наблюдая, как контур вокруг него полностью разгорелся.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a medium close-up against a neutral dark background. The person sits still, face and shoulders fully visible throughout, natural skin tone and clothing color unchanged.

Camera and motion structure:
Keep the overall shot completely static. No zoom, no camera movement — the growing outline glow is the only motion:
1. FRAMES 0–20%: A faint sliver of rainbow-gradient light (red→orange→yellow→green→blue→purple) glows along the bottom edge of the person's shoulders, barely visible, everything else natural and unchanged.
2. FRAMES 20–70%: The thin rainbow glow visibly climbs upward along the outer edge of the person's silhouette — tracing up the shoulders, along the jawline, and around the hairline — like a neon rim-light switching on segment by segment, never spreading inward onto the skin or clothing itself.
3. FRAMES 70–90%: The rainbow outline glow completes, fully tracing the person's entire silhouette edge from shoulders to hairline in a smooth, continuous, glowing line, cycling gently through its colors.
4. FRAMES 90–100%: The person's expression settles into a pleased, quiet smile, admiring the completed glow around them without looking away from camera. Hold on this moment to the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video — the rainbow glow traces only the outer silhouette edge and never recolors the skin, hair, or clothing itself.

Key visual events (in order):
- The faint first sliver of glow at the shoulders
- The glow climbing the silhouette's edge from shoulders to hairline (the hero moment)
- The fully completed, continuous rainbow outline around the whole silhouette
- The pleased, quiet smile at the end

Style:
Clean, dark-background neon-rim-light aesthetic — the glow reads like a light source outlining the person, not a color filter over them. Calm, proud, quietly striking.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's face, hair, skin tone, or clothing color at any point — only the glowing outline is added
- The person's face must remain centered, legible, and the clear visual focus throughout
- No speaking, no singing, no lip-sync, no mouthing words — the quiet smile is the only silent reaction
- No speech bubbles
- The rainbow glow must stay confined to a thin line tracing the silhouette's outer edge — it must never spread inward to recolor skin, hair, or clothing, and must never form a separate ring or border around the whole photo
- The rainbow gradient must remain smooth and continuous, cycling in color order, never flashing or reversing
- No jump cuts — the entire sequence plays as one continuous, unhurried take
- No other people in frame
- No text captions or overlay graphics
