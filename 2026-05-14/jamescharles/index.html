#jamescharles

Макияж материализуется

Пользователь в кадре без макияжа — чистое лицо, нейтральный фон в стиле студийного бьюти-съёмки. Камера стоит статично, мягкий свет. Затем начинается трансформация: невидимые кисти наносят макияж прямо в кадре — тени материализуются на веках, хайлайтер вспыхивает на скулах, яркая помада проявляется на губах. Всё это происходит плавно и спектакулярно, как будто кто-то рисует шедевр. В финале — портретный крупный план с оверлеем «SERVING LOOKS ✦» и значком палитры в углу.

Generate a cinematic still frame: a close-up portrait of the person in a beauty studio setting, clean face without any makeup.

Background: seamless pale lavender-white studio backdrop, very soft and out of focus.
Lighting: professional beauty photography ring light — even, flattering, soft shadows under the cheekbones, catch lights in the eyes. A subtle warm key light from a 45-degree angle adds dimension.
No makeup brushes, no props visible in frame.
The person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity must be preserved exactly from the reference photo.
Color grading: clean, bright, high-key beauty editorial — soft whites, natural skin tones, slight pastel warmth.
Style: photorealistic, high-end beauty campaign photography. Think Vogue Beauty or James Charles YouTube thumbnail aesthetic.
No overlay graphics in this frame — they will appear later.
Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical (TikTok format).
Промпт для видео Kling · Grok 
Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a professional beauty studio. The person from the image faces the camera in a clean close-up portrait — bare face, no makeup.

Camera and motion structure:
The camera is completely static throughout — a locked beauty close-up:
1. FRAMES 0–15%: Close-up portrait. The person's face is clean and natural. Soft studio lighting. They hold a calm, neutral gaze into camera. A single slow blink — the only movement.
2. FRAMES 15–45%: The makeup begins to materialize on its own — as if invisible professional brushes are at work. First: deep jewel-toned eyeshadow (purple-bronze gradient) builds up on the eyelids — appearing as soft brushstrokes of pigment forming from nothing. The person's eyes stay partially closed during this phase, then open to reveal the color.
3. FRAMES 45–70%: The transformation accelerates: warm golden highlighter blooms across the cheekbones with a prismatic light-scatter effect. Precise contour shadow defines the jawline. False eyelashes materialize lash by lash, extending and curling. The person's expression shifts from neutral to quietly stunning.
4. FRAMES 70–85%: Vivid cherry-red lipstick traces onto the lips in a single smooth stroke, as if a lip brush moved across them. The person's lips press together once — sealing the color.
5. FRAMES 85–100%: Full beat face. The person looks directly into camera with a powerful, polished expression. Two overlay graphics fade in as screen-locked elements:
   — Center bottom: "SERVING LOOKS ✦" in clean bold sans-serif with a subtle shimmer animation
   — Top corner: a small makeup palette icon with glowing pans
   Both overlays remain until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their facial structure, bone structure, or identity.
Allowed changes: makeup products applied (eyeshadow, highlighter, contour, lashes, lipstick) — these are additions, not changes to identity. Expression evolves from neutral to confident.
Forbidden: any change to eye shape, nose, lips shape, or facial structure.

Key visual events (in order):
- Eyeshadow materializing stroke by stroke on the eyelids
- Highlighter blooming on cheekbones (the prismatic light-scatter — the hero moment)
- False lashes extending lash by lash
- Lipstick tracing in one smooth stroke
- Final reveal with overlays

Style:
Elevated beauty editorial. Everything is controlled, luxurious, slow-build satisfaction. The materialization of each product should feel tactile and real — not a morph or dissolve, but actual makeup visibly being applied by an unseen hand. Lavender-white tones dominate the background; the jewel makeup colors pop.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's facial structure, bone structure, eye shape, nose, or lips shape
- Do not morph or distort the face at any point
- The "SERVING LOOKS ✦" overlay and palette icon appear only after FRAME 85% and must remain fully visible until the last frame
- No speech bubbles
- No social-media UI overlays (no TikTok hearts, comments, follower counts)
- No text captions other than the specified overlay
- No extra hands or visible makeup brushes in frame — the application is invisible/magical
- No jump cuts between makeup states — all transitions must be smooth and continuous
- No aggressive or violent actions
- Background must remain the seamless studio backdrop throughout — no scene changes
