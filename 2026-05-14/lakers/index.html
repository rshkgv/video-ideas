Тебя нашла камера

Арена «Крипто Арена» во время матча плей-офф НБА — зал бурлит, баскетбольное кольцо светится в дальнем плане, фиолетово-золотые флаги Lakers реют повсюду. Камера ведёт себя как настоящий телетрансляционный оператор: медленно проезжает по трибунам и «находит» пользователя среди тысяч болельщиков. Он вскакивает с места — кричит, вскидывает руки — и в этот момент трансляция берёт его крупным планом. В финале на экране появляется табло-оверлей «FAN OF THE GAME» с логотипом Lakers и золотыми вспышками конфетти.

Create a short cinematic video based on the uploaded image.
The scene is a packed NBA arena during a Los Angeles Lakers playoff game. The person from the image is seated among thousands of purple-and-gold fans in the lower bowl.

Camera and motion structure:
The video has a dramatic arc built entirely on camera movement — a broadcast operator hunting for a fan:
1. FRAMES 0–25%: Wide establishing shot. The arena roars. Flags wave, the scoreboard blazes. The person is barely visible among thousands of fans. The camera begins a slow, deliberate lateral pan and push-in along the lower bowl, hunting the crowd.
2. FRAMES 25–55%: Medium shot. The camera locks onto the person's section and begins a controlled zoom-in. The person starts to sense the camera finding them — their expression shifts from cheering to surprised recognition. They begin to rise from their seat.
3. FRAMES 55–80%: Close-up. The person is now standing, arms exploding upward in a scream. Purple-and-gold confetti erupts from above. The camera holds tight on their face — pure playoff energy.
4. FRAMES 80–100%: The close-up holds. A broadcast-style graphic smoothly slides up from the bottom: a purple-gold scoreboard bar reading "FAN OF THE GAME 🏆" with the Lakers shield logo on the left and a flashing gold star animation. The graphic stays until the last frame.

Character:
Preserve the person's face, hair, skin tone, and identity exactly from the source image throughout the entire video. Do not alter their appearance.
The person transitions from seated cheering → rising in excitement → arms-up screaming celebration. No other character movement.

Atmosphere and detail:
- Arena crowd alive throughout: waving flags, jumping, clapping
- Purple-and-gold confetti drops from the rafters, increasing density at the close-up moment
- Scoreboard visible in background during wide shot, switching to a "LAKERS LEAD" animation mid-video
- Broadcast-style overlay banding and score ticker at the very bottom edge during wide shot (disappears when the "FAN OF THE GAME" bar appears)
- Courtside LED boards pulsing gold during the climax
- Lens flare from arena floodlights shifting as camera pans

Style:
Cinematic NBA broadcast. The camera move should feel exactly like a real ESPN floor-level operator catching a fan mid-celebration — motivated, smooth, broadcast-quality. Purple-gold color palette dominates. Epic, loud, celebratory.

Restrictions:
- Do not change the person's appearance, face, clothing, or identity at any point
- The "FAN OF THE GAME" broadcast graphic appears only after FRAME 80% and must remain fully visible until the last frame
- Do not fade out or obscure the graphic once it appears
- No speech bubbles or dialogue text
- No social-media UI overlays
- No text captions other than the "FAN OF THE GAME" bar and the background scoreboard
- No extra characters added to the foreground
- No violent or aggressive actions
- Confetti and flags only — no pyrotechnics or fire
- The basketball court must remain visible in the background during wide and medium shots
